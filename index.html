About Us

Masjid Al-Muhajirin is dedicated to serving as a center for Islamic worship, education, and community development. We strive to provide a welcoming environment for Muslims and non-Muslims alike to learn about Islam and engage in meaningful dialogue.

We envision a vibrant, inclusive community that embodies the principles of Islam through worship, learning, and service. Our goal is to nurture spiritual growth, foster brotherhood and sisterhood, and contribute positively to the broader society.
